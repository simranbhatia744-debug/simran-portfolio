About

Mumbai-based costume stylist and designer building wardrobes that tell stories — across blockbuster films, streaming originals, national campaigns, and music videos.

Credits span Brahmastra, Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar, Showtime, and Nadaaaniyan, alongside campaigns and celebrity styling for Ranbir Kapoor, KL Rahul, Athiya Shetty, Rajkummar Rao, Vedang Raina, Tiger Shroff, and many more.

Trained under the mentorship of Anaita Shroff Adajania, one of India's most celebrated fashion directors.
